Legends

"Boby Fischer teaches Chess"

is a beginner-friendly guide to chess, written by the legendary grandmaster Bobby Fischer. The book focuses on teaching the fundamentals of the game, including basic tactics and strategies, through a series of puzzles and step-by-step lessons. It's designed to help new players quickly improve their skills and gain a deeper understanding of the game..

Chess Fundamentals" by José Raúl Capablanca

Capablanca, a former world chess champion, provides a timeless guide for beginners. The book covers the basics of the game, including essential strategies, piece movement, and endgames, all with a simple and effective teaching approach.

"The Steps Method" by Rob Brunia and Cor van Wijgerden

"This book is part of a series that introduces chess through a structured step-by-step approach, focusing on understanding the logic behind the moves. It’s ideal for beginners who want to build strategic thinking and develop a deep understanding of the game.











Helpful Defense Strategies

Атака жареной печени (Fried Liver Attack) Дебют: Итальянская партия Описание: Это агрессивная тактика, которая атакует короля противника на раннем этапе. Цель — вскрыть позицию короля и получить материальное преимущество. Ходы: e4 e5 Nf3 Nc6 Bc4 Nf6 Ng5 После этого угрожает мат на f7. Если черные берут коня (4...Nxe4), вы играете 5. d4 и быстро развиваете атаку.
